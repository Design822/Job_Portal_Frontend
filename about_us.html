About Us

VNVNEPAL was created over a decade ago in Nepal. Our vision was and still to this day, is to provide an efficient and professional ethical experience not just for our clients but also to all our staff. VNVNEPAL supplies thousands of workers across Nepal and other parts of the world every year to our valued partners. At Vision, we always deliver by following a simple policy -- when it comes to our clients -- we Listen – Plan – Recruit & Deliver

At Vision & Value Overseas, we understand the importance of candidate sourcing in finding the best talent for your organization. While we employ effective and efficient strategies to source candidates, we consider it essential to maintain confidentiality in order to mitigate potential risks.

We uphold a strict commitment to ethical recruitment practices, which includes safeguarding the privacy and interests of both candidates and employers. By not disclosing our sourcing strategies publicly, we ensure the protection of our candidates' information and prevent any compromises that could arise from revealing sensitive details.

However, we value transparency in our partnerships and believe in open communication. If you are an employer seeking a deeper understanding of our sourcing strategies, we encourage you to reach out to us directly. By sharing your contact details, we can engage in a confidential conversation where we can discuss our sourcing methodologies in detail.

Rest assured that our team is well-versed in various sourcing techniques and utilizes a comprehensive approach to attract and engage top-tier talent. We tailor our strategies to your specific needs, industry requirements, and job specifications to ensure we identify the most suitable candidates for your organization.

By choosing Vision & Value Overseas, you can trust that our candidate sourcing process is grounded in ethical practices, confidentiality, and a commitment to finding the best talent for your organization's success.
